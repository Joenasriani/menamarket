# 01_PRODUCT_VISION

## Product
MENAMarket is a prediction-market platform focused on MENA users and globally relevant events, implemented with clear resolution rules, strong auditability, and controlled jurisdiction-based feature gating.

## Product goals
- deliver a real market product, not a demo
- support clear event discovery and trading workflows
- prioritize trust through explicit rules and verifiable outcomes
- create a modular architecture that can evolve from closed beta to production
- minimize operational ambiguity in wording, settlement, and resolution

## Core product surfaces
- public homepage and market discovery
- event and market pages
- wallet connection and account session layer
- order book and trade entry
- positions and portfolio
- market resolution and redemption
- admin market operations
- audit and surveillance tooling

## Launch philosophy
- accuracy and integrity over speed
- no unverified production claims
- no geography exposure until compliance logic exists
- no market creation until wording standards and resolution workflow exist
